Font Unit

Absolute Lengths

cm ---> centimeters[1cm]

mm ---> millimeters[10mm]

in ---> inches (1in = 96px = 2.54cm)[0.5in]

px ---> pixels (1px = 1/96th of 1in)[32px]

pt ---> point (1pt = 1/72 of 1in)[36pt]

pc ---> picas (1pc = 12pt)[3pc]

Relative Lengths

em ---> relative to the font-size of the element1[2em]

ex ---> relative to the x-height of the current font (rarely used)[4ex]

ch ---> relative to the width of the "0"(zero)[4ch]

rem ---> relative to font-size of the root element[2rem]
root element base font-size 16px ---> html {font-size: XXpx}

vw ---> relative to 1% of the width of the viewport[2vw]

vh ---> relative to 1% of the height of the viewport[2vh]

vmin ---> relative to 1% of viewport's smaller dimension[2vmin]

vmax ---> relative to 1% of viewport's larger dimension[2vmax]

% ---> relative to the parent element[100%]

Font size guidelines
root font-size default 16px ---> 10px

Optimal font sizes for desktop

Body text - 16px , 18px or 21px

Headings - 1.96 times larger than your body text [18px ---> 35px]

Subheadings - smaller than heading size [35px ---> 30px]

Optimal font sizes for mobile

Body text - 14px , 15px or 16px

Headings - 1.3 times larger than your body text [16px ---> 21px]

Subheadings - scaled down from the heading size [21px ---> 16px or 18px]
